About Boo
Welcome to Boo's Bakery
Simple home made baking with a personal touch

Hi and welcome to Boo’s Bakery. My name is Sam and I have always had a passion for baking. I love designing cakes and seeing them come to life in front of my eyes. I often create cakes and other baked goods for family members, friends and work events. Hearing that people enjoy my creations has encouraged me to share my passion with others and bring you Boo’s Bakery.

Boo's Bakery is a new bespoke cake baking company based in Milton Keynes. Being a new company, we specialise in keeping it simple but still delicious. Looking for a tray bake for a birthday or cookies to take into work? Look no further Boo's Bakery is the place for you.

We may like to keep things simple here at Boo’s but if you are looking for something a little more special then have no fear! Do you have a birthday or other special event coming up? We are also able to create bespoke cakes for you to make the day extra special. Just take a look at our Gallery below to see past creations for a little inspiration. Contact us using the contact form on the contact page and we will get back to you to start discussing your dream creation.
